Complexity Analysis

O(max(N, M)) Time Complexity

We iterate through both linked lists exactly once. The loop continues until the longest list (and any leftover carry) is completely processed. In this scenario, max(, ) determines the exact number of iterations.

O(max(N, M)) Space Complexity

The length of the new generated list is at most max(N, M) + 1. The extra +1 handles the edge case where the final addition produces an extra carry (e.g., 99 + 1 = 100).
