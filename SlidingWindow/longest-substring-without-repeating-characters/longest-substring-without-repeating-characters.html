Deconstructing the Problem

"Given a string s, find the length of the longest substring without repeating characters."

Test Your Own Substrings

Rule 1: Substring vs. Subsequence

The question strictly asks for a substring. A substring is a contiguous sequence of characters. You cannot skip letters. You must slice the string cleanly like a piece of cake.

Valid Substring ("wke")

All characters are right next to each other.

Invalid (Subsequence "pwke")

You skipped the second 'w'. This is a subsequence, not a substring.

Rule 2: No Repeating Characters

Inside your chosen contiguous slice, every single character must be unique. If a letter appears twice, the whole slice is immediately disqualified.
