Primary Objective

Compute the final battlefield state. Return an array of the surviving robots' HP in the exact sequence they were originally provided in the input data.

Architectural Logic

1

Spatial Sorting Crucial

Time flows sequentially across space. Because our input arrays are unsorted, evaluating them as-is is impossible. We must aggregate each robot's `(id, position, health, direction)` into a data packet, and sort them ascending by position (Left to Right).

Input: [P:5, P:2, P:8]
Sorted Line: [P:2] → [P:5] → [P:8]
2

The Stack Mechanism (LIFO)

As we scan from left to right:

Right-Movers ('R')

Pushed to a Stack. They fly to the right, waiting in limbo to see if they crash into a Left-mover later down the line.

Left-Movers ('L')

Instantly trigger collisions. They smash into the most recent Right-mover (the Top of the Stack).

3

Reassembling the Output

Once the dust settles, the Stack holds the sole survivors. However, the mission requires HP values ordered by their original input ID. We simply sort the remaining stack contents by `id` and map out the health values. Mission accomplished.
